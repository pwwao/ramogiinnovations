About Us

At Ramogi Innovations, our team is dedicated to advancing biomedical research.Ramogi Innovations focuses on applied research, technological innovation, and community-driven solutions that align with Kenya’s Vision 2030, the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), and Africa’s Agenda 2063. We emphasize on practical, market-driven skills and have been involved in various initiatives, including partnerships with international organizations such as Y factort(CBaas) to enhance skills development and sustainability within our country and beyond.

The team at Ramogi Innovations comprises experts in research, fieldwork, and laboratory sciences, working collaboratively to develop and implement solutions that address local and regional challenges.
